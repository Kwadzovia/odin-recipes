Pb&J

Description

You don't need a description for a pb&j.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam or Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter with a knife on one slice of bread
  2. Spread Jam or Jelly with a knife on another slice of bread
  3. Combine the two slices of bread, facing each other
